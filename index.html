Explore Tuscany's Natural Beauty

What you see above are the free thermal pools of Saturnia, just one of the several thermal sites you can visit in Tuscany.
Some of them became luxury SPA structures, some of them are immersed in nature, completely free to visit and enjoy.
Bathing in thermal water is one of the most famous ancient cures for spirit, skin and body.
We decided to bring it to you providing a map of all of the therma sites, SPA's and Natural Spring Waters,
together with some recommended restaurants and the major historical spots in each area.
